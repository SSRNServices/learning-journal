# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of this project seriously. If you believe you have found a security vulnerability, please report it to us via email rather than opening a public issue.

**Contact Email**: [ssrnservices@gmail.com](mailto:ssrnservices@gmail.com)

### Expected Response
- We will acknowledge receipt of your report within **48 hours**.
- We will provide a preliminary evaluation of the report shortly after.
- We will keep you updated on the progress of the fix.

### Responsible Disclosure
We ask that you follow responsible disclosure principles:
- Give us reasonable time to investigate and mitigate the issue before making any information public.
- Avoid violating privacy or disrupting services during your research.
